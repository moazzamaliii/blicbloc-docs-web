# Home Feed & Discovery

## Personalized Reader Stream

The BlicBloc Home Feed connects readers with content from creators they track alongside smart recommendations.

---

## Features

- **Tracked Stream**: See recent Blics and Sparks from MainSpaces you follow.
- **Explore & Trending**: Discover top creators, trending Blics, and category spotlights.
- **Global Search**: Search MainSpaces, creators, Blic titles, and topics in real-time.
- **Saved Items**: Bookmark Blics and Sparks to read later.
