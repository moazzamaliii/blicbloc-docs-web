# Creator Analytics & Insights

## Track Your Growth

Creator Space provides detailed metrics to help you understand your audience and optimize content strategy:

- **Total Views**: Overall reader views across Blics and Sparks.
- **Top Content**: See which Article, Video, or Project Blics drive the highest engagement.
- **Follower Growth**: Track subscriber count over time.
- **Resource Downloads**: Monitor how many visitors download attached PDFs, cheat sheets, or code assets.
