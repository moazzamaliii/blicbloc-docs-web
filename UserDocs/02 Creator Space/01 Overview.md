# Creator Space Overview

## Your Private Management Workspace

While MainSpace is what visitors see, **Creator Space** is your private management cockpit.

---

## What You Can Do in Creator Space

- **Publishing Editor**: Write long-form Blics with rich markdown support, image uploads, and resource attachments.
- **Spark Dispatcher**: Post instant Sparks to your MainSpace timeline.
- **Analytics Dashboard**: Monitor reader views, engagement, follower growth, and popular Blics.
- **MainSpace Customizer**: Adjust themes, custom domain settings, profile tabs, and button links.
- **Audience Communications**: Manage subscriber lists and direct creator communications.
