# What is a MainSpace?

## Your Central Digital Home

A **MainSpace** is your public profile page on BlicBloc. It acts as your website, portfolio, blog, and social hub—all wrapped into one clean, professional layout.

---

## Features of MainSpace

- **Custom Header & Hero**: Display your brand name, avatar, bio, and social links.
- **Content Tabs**: Organize your work into Blics, Sparks, Projects, Resources, and About tabs.
- **Visitor Tracking Button**: Allow visitors to track your MainSpace with one click.
- **Public Spark Stream**: Post quick updates directly on your MainSpace timeline.
- **Searchable Library**: Visitors can search your published Blics and resources easily.
