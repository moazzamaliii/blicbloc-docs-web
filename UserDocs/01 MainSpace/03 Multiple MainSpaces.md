# Creating Multiple MainSpaces

## One Account, Multiple Brands

You are not restricted to just one MainSpace. BlicBloc allows creators to manage multiple MainSpaces under a single account!

---

## Use Cases

- **MainSpace 1**: Personal Brand ()
- **MainSpace 2**: Freelance Agency ()
- **MainSpace 3**: Educational Course Hub ()
- **MainSpace 4**: Podcast or Community ()

Each MainSpace has its own independent content, custom domain, theme, and audience tracking list while managed easily from one Creator Space cockpit!
