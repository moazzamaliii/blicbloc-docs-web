# Frequently Asked Questions

## General Questions

### Q: Is BlicBloc free to use?
**A:** Yes! Any user can sign up, explore content, track creators, and launch a MainSpace with 5GB of storage for free.

### Q: Can I use my own custom domain?
**A:** Absolutely. You can connect your custom domain (e.g., ) or use your free BlicBloc subdomain ().

### Q: Can I create more than one MainSpace?
**A:** Yes! You can create multiple MainSpaces under a single account to manage separate personal, freelance, educational, or business brands.

### Q: What is the difference between a Blic and a Spark?
**A:** A **Blic** is a long-form durable asset (Article, Video, Project, Resource, Image). A **Spark** is a short-form instant update posted directly to your MainSpace timeline.
