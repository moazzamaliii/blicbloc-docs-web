# What are Sparks?

## Fast, Casual Communication

While Blics are for long-form durable work, **Sparks** are lightweight short-form updates published instantly to your MainSpace timeline.

---

## Why Use Sparks?

- **Quick Announcements**: Share product updates, milestones, or news.
- **Lightweight Thoughts**: Post casual ideas without writing a full article.
- **Audience Pulse**: Ask questions and keep your followers engaged daily.
- **Link Sharing**: Share interesting articles or tools with a brief comment.
