# Spark Formats & Best Practices

## 4 Spark Types

1. **Text Spark**: Concise written thoughts or announcements.
2. **Image Spark**: Quick photos, diagrams, or visual previews.
3. **Video Spark**: Short video clips or teaser previews.
4. **Link Spark**: Curated link shares with custom commentary.

---

## When to use Blics vs Sparks?

- Use **Blics** for comprehensive, durable content you want indexed and read for years.
- Use **Sparks** for fast, real-time updates and everyday audience interactions!
