# Who Uses BlicBloc?

BlicBloc is designed for anyone who wants to build a professional digital presence, share knowledge, and grow an audience.

---

## Creator Types

- **🧑‍🏫 Teachers & Educators**: Publish learning guides, educational videos, study materials, and communicate with students.
- **💼 Freelancers**: Showcase past work, display client services, publish case studies, and convert visitors into clients.
- **💻 Developers**: Share open-source projects, post technical articles, upload code resources, and build a technical portfolio.
- **🎨 Designers**: Display UI/UX case studies, design resource kits, and showcase visual portfolios.
- **🚀 Startups & Businesses**: Share product announcements, publish dev logs, build a customer community, and manage brand identity.
- **📖 Writers & Content Creators**: Combine deep-dive articles with casual audience updates without managing multiple newsletters.
