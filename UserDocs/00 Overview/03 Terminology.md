# Platform Terminology

Understanding BlicBloc terms makes navigating the platform simple and intuitive:

| Term | Definition |
|------|------------|
| **MainSpace** | The public creator profile and digital home page visible to visitors. |
| **Creator Space** | The private management hub where creators write content, view analytics, and customize settings. |
| **Blic** | The primary unit of long-form, durable content (Articles, Videos, Projects, Resources, Images). |
| **Spark** | Short-form instant updates published directly from MainSpace for fast communication. |
| **Home Feed** | The central discovery stream showing posts from creators you track and recommended content. |
| **Tracking** | Subscribing to a creator's MainSpace to receive updates in your Home Feed and Notifications. |
