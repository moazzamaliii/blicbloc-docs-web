# Understanding Blics

## Durable, Structured Content

A **Blic** is the primary unit of long-form, professional content in BlicBloc. Unlike social media posts that vanish, Blics are built for durability, searchability, and educational value.

---

## 5 Types of Blics

1. **Article Blic**: Deep-dive writing with markdown, table of contents, and code syntax highlighting.
2. **Video Blic**: Video hosting with transcript support, chapter navigation, and attached materials.
3. **Project Blic**: Interactive portfolio showcase for open-source code, design case studies, or products.
4. **Resource Blic**: Downloadable asset library (PDFs, templates, design kits, cheat sheets).
5. **Image Blic**: Visual galleries and photography showcases.
