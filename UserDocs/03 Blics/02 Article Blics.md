# Article Blics

## Powerful Publishing Environment

Article Blics provide a clean, distraction-free reading experience for tutorials, essays, research papers, and technical guides.

---

## Features

- **Rich Markdown Format**: Headings, lists, blockquotes, tables, and links.
- **Code Blocks & Copy Button**: Formatted syntax highlighting for code snippets.
- **Estimated Reading Time**: Automatic reading time indicator for readers.
- **Table of Contents**: Auto-generated sub-heading navigation sidebar.
- **Attached Downloads**: Option to attach downloadable files to any article.
